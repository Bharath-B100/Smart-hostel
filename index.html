Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit mess feedback?

To submit feedback, go to the Mess Feedback tab and click on "Submit Feedback". Select the meal type, rate the food quality, add any comments, and click submit.

How do I apply for leave?

Go to the Leave Application tab, click on "Apply for Leave", fill in the required details including leave type, dates, and reason, then submit your application.

How can I report a hostel issue?

Navigate to the Hostel Reports tab, click on "Report Issue", select the appropriate category, provide location and description, set urgency level, and submit your report.

How do I mark attendance?

Go to the Attendance tab, select the date and attendance type, mark students as present or absent, then click "Save Attendance".

How can I register a visitor?

Navigate to the Visitors tab, fill in the visitor details including name, purpose, and student being visited, then submit the form.
