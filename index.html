Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit mess feedback?

To submit feedback, go to the Mess Feedback tab and click on "Submit Feedback". Select the meal type, rate the food quality and menu variety, add any comments, and click submit.

How do I apply for leave?

Go to the Leave Application tab, click on "Apply for Leave", fill in the required details including leave type, dates, and reason, then submit your application.

How can I report a hostel issue?

Navigate to the Hostel Reports tab, click on "Report Issue", select the appropriate category, provide location and description, set urgency level, and submit your report.

Who can see my feedback and reports?

Your feedback is anonymous to other students. Only the mess management team can see which feedback belongs to which student. Reports are visible to hostel administration for resolution.

How do I change my profile picture?

Go to the Settings page, and in the Profile Settings section, you can upload a new profile picture using the "Choose File" button and then click "Upload Picture".
